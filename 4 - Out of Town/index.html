Outside of Moscow

Here are some other places that I visited in Russia that I thought were amazing and would recommend to others!! Click through and see what kind of stuff you like!

1 / 5
Saint Petersburg is one of the most iconic cities in Russia. This will be an amazing trip and I highly recommend!
2 / 5
Ekaterinburg is worth a visit - and if you go in the depths of winter it is especially magical!
3 / 5
Nizny Novgorod is one of the most historic towns in Russia and features many beautiful churches! Summer is a lovely time to visit!
4 / 5
Kazan is a breathtaking city and one of my favourite places in Moscow. There is some pretty mindblowing architecture and an amazing panorama point which I highly recommend!
5 / 5
Sergiev Pasad is a very easy day trip from Moscow and a highly religious place in Russian history! You can walk around the main complex and take in the amazing orthodox architecture and ambience!
