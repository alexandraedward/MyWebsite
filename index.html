Welcome to my website about Moscow!

Moscow is the capital and most heavily populated city in Russia, as well as being the largest city on the European continent. It is an iconic and global city and is the key political, economic, cultural and scientific centre of not only Russia but of Eastern Europe as well. It is a city renowned for the Red Square, the Kremlin and St. Basil’s cathedral and furthermore, should be on your travel bucket list

I was so lucky to live in Moscow for a year as part of my degree and want to start this website to give some tips and hints to those who are planning on visiting, either just for their holidays or as part of their studies!!

I'm an undergrad going into my final year and I study Russian and IR. I'm super passionate about travel and needing to see the world in order to understand it. Russia seems like a far away place that is hard to get. To some extent, that is true. But I firmly believe it is worth making the effort to get to know the country and everything it has to offer

So check out my website!!
